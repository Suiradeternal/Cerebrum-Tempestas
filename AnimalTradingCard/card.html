Immortal Jellyfish

The Immortal Jellyfish has a virtually limitless lifespan due to its ability to reverse its biotic cycle.

  • Scientific Name: Turritopsis dohrnii
  • Average Size: 4.5mm
  • Lifespan: Virtually Immortal
  • Habitat: Temperate to tropical waters

The Immortal Jellyfish is one of the few known cases of animals capable of reverting completely to a sexually immature, colonial stage after having reached sexual maturity as a solitary individual.It usually happens as a response to stress or other environmental trigger.
